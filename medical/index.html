WHY CHOOSE US

Founded by Stanford alumni, we are an online service that helps you connect with medical specialists from the US. We believe that medical expertise from top US hospitals is not just for the rich, but is for everyone.
斯坦福校友创立的线上问诊服务，帮助您连接美国的医疗专家。我们相信，美国顶级医院的医疗服务，不止为富人服务，也可以为大众服务。

Expert Consultation

We help you collect all your relevant medical information, reformat them into a medical presentation in English, and send your questions along with your medical information to top-tier US hospitals.
我们帮助您采集您的医疗信息。通过我们的专业背景，我们帮您整理和归纳您的病情。我们将您的医疗问题和信息送至美国顶尖医院。

Fast

We obtain an expert opinion for your medical case in 1-2 weeks, and transform the medical information into a useful format for you.
